About Me

I'm a Biomedical Engineer graduated from Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB) — and since day one, I've believed that the best engineers never really stop being students. My world sits at the intersection of electronics, diagnostics, and AI, where the goal is always the same: make healthcare faster, smarter, and more human.

From deploying 73 medical devices across 32 healthcare facilities, to building a biosensor that detects cardiac troponin with 100% accuracy, I care deeply about the full journey — from lab bench all the way to the patient's bedside.

Outside the technical side, I've led teams, written SOPs, and managed programs spanning hundreds of people — because as any good engineer knows, the hardest system to debug is always the human one.
